# Forward Simulations

## Existing simulation tools
[**SFS_Code**](http://sfscode.sourceforge.net/SFS_CODE/index/index.html)
[Hernandez RD (2008) A flexible forward simulator for populations subject to selection and demography. Bioinformatics 24: 2786-2787.](http://bioinformatics.oxfordjournals.org/content/24/23/2786.short)

[**fwdpp**](https://github.com/molpopgen/fwdpp)
[Thornton KR (2014) A C++ template library for efficient forward-time population genetic simulation of large populations. Genetics 198: 157-166.](http://www.genetics.org/content/198/1/157)

[**forwsim**](http://sourceforge.net/projects/forwsim/files/forwsim.c/download)
[Padhukasahasram B, Marjoram P, Wall JD, Bustamante CD, Nordborg M (2008) Exploring population genetic models with recombination using efficient forward-time simulations. Genetics 178: 2417-2427.](http://dx.doi.org/10.1534/genetics.107.085332)

[**forqs**](https://bitbucket.org/dkessner/forqs)
[Kessner D, Novembre J (2014) forqs: forward-in-time simulation of recombination, quantitative traits and selection. Bioinformatics 30: 576-577.](http://bioinformatics.oxfordjournals.org/content/30/4/576.abstract)

[**Fregene**](http://www.ebi.ac.uk/sites/ebi.ac.uk/files/groups/kersey_service_team/FREGEN/documentation_html.html)
[Chadeau-Hyam M, et al.(2008) Fregene: Simulation of realistic sequence-level data in populations and ascertained samples. BMC Bioinformatics 9:364  doi:10.1186/1471-2105-9-364.](http://www.biomedcentral.com/1471-2105/9/364/) 

[**Nemo 2.0**](http://nemo2.sourceforge.net/)
[Guillaume F, Rougemont J (2006) Nemo: an evolutionary and population genetics programming framework.Bioinformatics  Oct 15;22(20):2556-7.](http://bioinformatics.oxfordjournals.org/content/22/20/2556.abstract)
